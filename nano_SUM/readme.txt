The KE_PFO script offers:

1. Fit to your data three types of equations: logaritmic, first order and Kalapus equation (KE) describe in the manuscript.
2. Determination coefficient R2 and parameters of every equation.
3. Visualization of this three equations and comparision with orginal data.
4. Feature to estimate dependent variable with increase of independent variable. There is also the possibility to find y at any given x by use of the script.
